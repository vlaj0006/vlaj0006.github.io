HTML tags i've learnt

h1
'h' is the heading tag and 'h1' is the biggest size out of the 6 sizes.
p
'p' is the paragraph tag used to write text.
q
'q' is the quotation tag used to add quotations on text.
em
'em' is the emphasis tag used to add italics to text
mark
'mark' is the highlighting tag used to highlight text.
